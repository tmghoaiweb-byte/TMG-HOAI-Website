About the Platform

Built for clarity, transparency, and community trust

This website reduces reliance on scattered group chats by keeping important HOA information in one organized place.

Why this website matters

TMG HOAI needs a clean and accessible official platform where homeowners can review announcements, records, projects, and payment instructions without searching through multiple chats.

The design is made for all age groups: large text, clear navigation, readable sections, and fast-loading static files.

  • Central source for official updates
  • Permanent archive for records and documents
  • Organized concern and request submission
  • Clear visibility of projects and community plans
